# Content Strategy

Goal: convince both engineers and recruiters that `marketdata_cpp` is a real, functioning system.

Principles:
- Show proof first (run status, deterministic check, key metrics)
- Keep visuals autoplay/passive to reduce friction
- Always expose raw artifact links for auditability
- Explain why each metric/check matters in plain language

Page roles:
- Home: one-screen proof summary
- Replay: visual market-state evidence
- Performance: latency/throughput interpretation
- Correctness: deterministic and invariant evidence
- Architecture: pipeline and provenance
- Artifacts: raw downloadable files
